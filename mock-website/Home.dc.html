What we run, year-round

Not just one day in February — community, all year.

Language Circles

Weekly meetups where neighbours teach each other a few words of their mother tongue over tea.

Youth Ambassadors

Teens run workshops teaching younger kids songs and stories from their family's language.

Storytelling Nights

Monthly evenings of folktales, poetry and lullabies performed live in their original language.
